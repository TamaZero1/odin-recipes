Homemade Lasagna Sheets

Homemade lasagna noodles are easier to make than you think and you don't even need a pasta machine.
And forget about blanching the sheets before baking — no precooking is required!

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups Italian-style tipo 00 flour, plus additional for dusting
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon water as needed

Directions

Step 1
Gather all ingredients.



Step 2
Place flour on a marble or wooden work surface.
Make a well in the center and crack in eggs; add salt.



Step 3
Gently beat eggs using a fork, incorporating surrounding flour, until mixture is runny.
Bring remaining flour into the mixture using a bench scraper until dough forms a ball.
Mix in water, 1 tablespoon at a time, if dough feels too stiff.
